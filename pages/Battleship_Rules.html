Battleship Game Rules

Learn how to play the classic Battleship game

Objective

The goal of Battleship is to sink all of your opponent's ships before they sink yours!

Setup

Each player has a 10x10 grid. You will place ships on the grid secretly. The grid and ships are described below:

  • Carrier - 5 squares
  • Battleship - 4 squares
  • Cruiser - 3 squares
  • Submarine - 3 squares
  • Destroyer - 2 squares

Gameplay

Players take turns calling out coordinates (e.g., "A5") to hit their opponent's ships.

Hit or Miss:

  • Hit - A shot hits a ship.
  • Miss - The shot misses the ship.

Once a ship is hit on all its squares, it is sunk. For example: "You sank my Battleship!"

End of Game

The game ends when a player sinks all of the opponent's ships. The first player to do this wins!

Additional Rules

  1. No targeting the same spot twice.
  2. Ships cannot touch each other; they must have at least one square of water between them.
